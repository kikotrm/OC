Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to the most common questions below.

1. How can I change my password? +
  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. Click the "Recover Password" button.
  3. An email will be sent to your registered address with instructions to reset your password.
  4. Follow the instructions in the email to complete the process.
2. How do I edit my username? +
  1. Go to your profile page.
  2. Click the "Edit Profile" button.
  3. Change your username in the appropriate field.
  4. Click "Save" to apply the changes.
3. How do I contact support? +
To contact support, click the "Support" tab in the top menu and fill out the contact form. Our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
4. Is my data safe on OpenCourt? +
Yes! We take security seriously. We use secure Firebase authentication and encrypted storage to protect your personal data.
